Welcome to WHVFD

The West Hartland Volunteer Fire Department (WHVFD) is a 100% volunteer organization established in 1930, providing fire fighting, rescue, and limited medical services to the Hartland community. The department is committed to protecting the lives and property of residents through various emergency services, including fire protection and rescue operations. They hold monthly meetings and welcome prospective members interested in joining their team.

Who We Are

The WHVFD is located at 152 Center Street, in West hartland. We provide primary fire supression within the town, as well as supplemental first responders for Winsted Ambulance, and part of a district wide tanker task force.
