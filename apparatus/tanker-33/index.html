Specifications

  • Year: 2009

  • Type: Tanker
  • Tank: 3000gal polypropylene tank
  • Pump: 500gpm Hale pump

Role in the Department

Tanker 33 is the most likely apparatus to be used out of town. Many departments in the area are a part of a tanker task force, so if there is a large structure fire, engines at the scene are able to have a constant supply of water available.
