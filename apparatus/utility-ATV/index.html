ATV

Overview

The ATV, or Quad, is the primary Search and Rescue vehicle for the department. It is able to tow the ResQ trailer, and can be used to assist in brush fire knockdown.

Specifications

  • Year:2006
  • Make/Model: Suzuki Vinson ATV
  • Type: All Terrain Vehicle

Role in the Department

Out ATV is our primary search and rescue (SAR) vehicle, and is equiped to tow a stokes basket trailer.
